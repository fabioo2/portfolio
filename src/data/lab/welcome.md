# Welcome to the Lab

This is a space for half-baked ideas, proof-of-concept experiments, and notes I want to keep public-ish but not necessarily polish into anything.

## What lives here

- **Thoughts** — short essays, takes, and observations from day-to-day engineering work
- **POCs** — working code samples and live demos
- **Random** — links, snippets, and things that don't fit elsewhere

## Why not a blog?

Calling it a "blog" implies a level of commitment to publishing that I don't want to make. This is just a notebook with the door left open.
